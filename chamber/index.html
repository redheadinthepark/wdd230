Current Events

Farmers Market

Our farmer's market runs every Friday evening 4pm-9pm, April-October.

Mesa's farmers market is a vibrant gathering place where local growers and artisans showcase their best offerings. From ripe Arizona-grown fruits and vegetables, to handmade crafts, local honey, and unique culinary treats, you will find what you are looking for. Set against the backdrop of Mesa's sunny climate, the market embodies a lively atmosphere that celebrates both sustainability and community spirit. 8714 Garden Gate Blvd, Mesa, AZ

Demolition Derby

Don't miss this 2 night only event running July 12 & 13th! Gates open at 5 pm, purchase tickets at the door.

Mesa's demolition derby is a thrilling spectacle that draws crowds from across the region. Held annually, this event showcases the adrenaline-pumping collision of skill and destruction as drivers maneuver their vehicles through chaotic obstacles. Spectators are treated to a high-energy atmosphere filled with roaring engines, daring maneuvers, and the occasional spectacular crash. It's a beloved tradition, bringing together an unforgettable display of raw horsepower and controlled chaos. Mesa Events Center, 1254 Rockridge Blvd., Mesa, AZ
